Latest News

Ukraine Crisis

The Ukraine crisis, which started in 2014, has already forced at least 1.5 million people from their homes. The recent escalation in hostilities in Ukraine has put at least 7.5 million children in grave danger of physical harm, severe emotional distress displacement.

Afghanistan Crisis

As the United States begins welcoming newly arriving Afghan refugees, Save the Children has mobilized to meet their most urgent needs. We stand committed to the Afghan people and our commitment to stay and deliver in Afghanistan, where we've been working since 1976.

The Climate Crisis

The threat posed to children and their rights by the climate crisis is real, and it is urgent. Its impacts are built into children’s futures, even if we are able to curb global emissions.

Haiti Emergency

The earthquake in Haiti has created a critically dangerous situation for children and families, many of whom were already experiencing extreme poverty, hunger, and violence.

Yemen Crisis

In war-torn Yemen, 85,000 children may have died from starvation since the start of the crisis. As the largest aid organization in Yemen, Save the Children is on the ground, working to help the most vulnerable survive and stay safe.

Syrian Conflict and Refugee Crisis

Syria has been a living nightmare for children and families tormented by years of war and unimaginable hardship. As millions flee for their lives, your help is desperately needed.

Lebanon Crisis

After a devastating explosion in the Lebanese capital of Beirut, children and families already facing COVID-19, as well as economic and political crisis, are more vulnerable than ever.
